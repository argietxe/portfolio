About Me

Hey, my name is Argi !

I am a Data Analyst, Project Manager, and Creative Specialist based in Berlin, Germany. My background spans diverse fields including design research, visual arts, and set design, which has honed my ability to think critically and creatively. I've recently shifted my focus to data analysis, where I blend my analytical skills with my artistic sensibilities to create engaging and informative visualizations.

I thrive on creativity and versatility, adapting quickly to various work environments and team dynamics. My experience includes mapping out regions, creating interactive digital platforms, and working with local communities to uncover and share stories. My passion lies in using data to uncover patterns, analyze trends, and communicate insights clearly through interactive dashboards and real-time data visualizations.

Projects

Sustainable Journeys

Analyzed and visualized tourism's impact on economies and environments from 2004 to 2020. Developed engaging maps and visualizations that were lauded for their informational, impactful, and poetic visuals.

Hostile Bodies, Research and Design Curation

Investigated the paradoxical relationship between the human body and the physicality of data centers. Developed a dual-platform comprising a publication and a website that showcases the research and interprets the function of data centers in 2020.

Symphony for the New Migration

Researched and designed a methodology for territorial research, exploring correlations between human, machine landscapes, and the biosphere. Developed a website for hosting the project description and visual archive.

Studio Maixu

Co-founded design studio working on conceptual research, visual identities, art direction, prop making, and set dressing.
